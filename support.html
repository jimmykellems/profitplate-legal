Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open the Settings app on your iPhone, tap your name at the top, then go to Subscriptions. Find ProfitPlate and tap Cancel Subscription. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period.

How do I delete my account and data?

Inside the app, go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. This permanently removes all your restaurant data from our servers. This action cannot be undone.

My data isn't syncing across devices. What should I do?

Make sure you are signed in to the same account on both devices. Pull down to refresh on any list screen. If the issue persists, sign out and sign back in from Settings → Account.

I lost my data after reinstalling. Can I recover it?

Yes. Sign in with the same account you used before and your data will sync back automatically. All your recipes, ingredients, and settings are stored in the cloud and tied to your account.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings inside the app and tap Restore Purchases. Make sure you are signed in to the Apple ID that was used for the original purchase.

Can I use ProfitPlate on multiple devices?

Yes. Sign in with the same account on any iOS device and your data will sync across all of them automatically.
