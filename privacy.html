Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 29, 2025

1. Information We Collect

ProfitPlate collects only the information necessary to provide the app's core features:

  • Account information: Your email address and authentication credentials when you sign in (via email/password or Apple Sign-In).
  • Restaurant data: Restaurant name, menu items, recipes, ingredients, and cost information you enter into the app.
  • Usage data: Basic app interaction data used to improve performance and stability.

We do not collect payment card numbers. Subscription billing is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to operate ProfitPlate:

  • To sync your restaurant data across devices using Firebase/Firestore (Google LLC).
  • To manage your subscription status via Apple's StoreKit framework.
  • To restore your data when you reinstall the app or sign in on a new device.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored locally on your device and synced to Firebase Firestore, a secure cloud database operated by Google LLC. Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (TLS/HTTPS).

We follow industry-standard practices to protect your data, but no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure.

4. Third-Party Services

ProfitPlate uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase / Firestore (Google LLC) — cloud data storage and authentication. See Google's Privacy Policy at policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Apple App Store / StoreKit — subscription billing and in-app purchases. See Apple's Privacy Policy at apple.com/legal/privacy.

These services operate under their own privacy policies and data practices.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may delete your account at any time from Settings → Account → Delete Account. Upon deletion, all your restaurant data is permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.

6. Children's Privacy

ProfitPlate is designed for use by restaurant owners and food service professionals. We do not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by updating the effective date at the top of this document. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
